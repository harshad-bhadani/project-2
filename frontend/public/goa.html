Goa

Historic Facts:

  • Goa is the smallest state in India, located on the western coast along the Arabian Sea.
  • It was a Portuguese colony until 1961, which greatly influences its culture and architecture.
  • Famous for its beautiful beaches, Goa attracts millions of tourists each year.
  • The state is also known for its vibrant nightlife, music festivals, and seafood cuisine.
  • Goa's churches, such as the Basilica of Bom Jesus, are UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
