Ajanta Caves

Highlights of Ajanta Caves:

  • Ajanta Caves are a group of 30 rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments carved into a 100-meter cliff.
  • These caves date back to the 2nd century BCE and showcase intricate sculptures and murals.
  • The caves are known for their stunning frescoes depicting the life of the Buddha.
  • Ajanta is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts visitors from around the globe.
  • The site offers a glimpse into ancient Indian art and architecture.
