Kerala

Scenic Highlights:

  • Kerala is renowned for its stunning backwaters and houseboats.
  • The state is famous for its lush green landscapes and tea gardens, especially in Munnar.
  • Kerala is known for its unique culture and traditions, including classical dance forms like Kathakali.
  • The cuisine is famous for its use of spices and coconut, with dishes like Kerala Sadya.
  • It is often referred to as "God's Own Country" due to its natural beauty and serene environment.
