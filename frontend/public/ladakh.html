Ladakh

Highlights of Ladakh:

  • Ladakh is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, including mountains and valleys.
  • The region is home to the stunning Pangong Lake, known for its mesmerizing blue waters.
  • Rich in culture, Ladakh has many ancient monasteries, such as Hemis and Thiksey.
  • The unique blend of Tibetan and Indian culture offers a rich experience for visitors.
  • Ladakh is also renowned for adventure activities like trekking, biking, and river rafting.
