Varanasi

Historic Facts:

  • Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is known as the spiritual capital of India.
  • It is situated on the banks of the River Ganges and is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus.
  • The city is famous for its ghats, where pilgrims perform rituals and ceremonies.
  • Varanasi is home to numerous temples, including the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • The city is also known for its silk weaving industry and rich cultural heritage.
