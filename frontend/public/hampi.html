Hampi

Highlights of Hampi:

  • Virupaksha Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is a stunning example of ancient architecture.
  • The ruins of Hampi are a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for their historical significance.
  • Hampi Bazaar offers a glimpse into the vibrant markets of the Vijayanagara Empire.
  • Lotus Mahal is famous for its unique lotus-shaped arches and is a must-visit.
  • The landscape is dotted with giant boulders and lush greenery, making it a paradise for trekkers.
