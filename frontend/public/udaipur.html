Udaipur

Highlights of Udaipur:

  • Known as the "City of Lakes," Udaipur is famous for its stunning lakes and palaces.
  • The City Palace offers breathtaking views and intricate architecture.
  • Lake Pichola, a picturesque lake, is perfect for boat rides and offers views of the city.
  • Visit Jag Mandir, a beautiful island palace situated on Lake Pichola.
  • The annual Udaipur World Music Festival showcases a blend of music and culture.
