Mysore

Highlights of Mysore:

  • Mysore Palace is a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture and a must-visit landmark.
  • Known for its vibrant festivals, especially the grand Dasara festival celebrated with great pomp.
  • Chamundi Hill offers breathtaking views of the city and is home to the iconic Chamundeshwari Temple.
  • Mysore Zoo is one of the oldest and well-maintained zoos in India, showcasing diverse wildlife.
  • The city is also famous for Mysore silk, sandalwood products, and delicious Mysore pak sweets.
