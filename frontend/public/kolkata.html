Kolkata

Highlights of Kolkata:

  • The Victoria Memorial is a magnificent structure and a museum dedicated to Queen Victoria.
  • Howrah Bridge, an iconic symbol of Kolkata, connects the city to its suburbs.
  • The Dakshineswar Kali Temple is a revered pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Kali.
  • Kolkata is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant festivals, and delicious street food.
  • The city is also famous for its literary history, with many renowned authors hailing from here.
